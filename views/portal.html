❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How do I connect to WiFi? ▼
Select a package on the Connect tab, enter your M-Pesa number, approve the payment prompt on your phone, and you'll be automatically connected in seconds.
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
We accept M-Pesa via STK Push — a payment request sent directly to your phone. Just enter your PIN when prompted.
What happens when my package expires? ▼
Internet access stops automatically and you'll be redirected here to buy a new package. Nothing on your device is deleted.
I was charged but have no internet! ▼
Go to the Reconnect tab, enter your M-Pesa reference code from your confirmation SMS, and tap Verify & Connect. Still stuck? Call 0796 820 013.
What speeds can I expect? ▼
Speeds depend on your package and current network load. Plans range from 3 Mbps (30-min) up to 20 Mbps (Monthly). Speeds are shared across connected users.

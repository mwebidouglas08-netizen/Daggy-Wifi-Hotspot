❓ FAQ
How do I connect to WiFi? ▼
Select a package, enter your M-Pesa number, approve the phone prompt, and you're connected in seconds.
What payment methods work? ▼
We accept M-Pesa via STK Push — a prompt sent to your phone. Just enter your PIN.
What happens when my package expires? ▼
Internet stops and you'll be redirected here to buy a new package.
Charged but no internet? ▼
Go to Reconnect tab, enter your M-Pesa reference from your SMS, and tap Verify & Connect. Still stuck? Call 0796820013.
What speeds can I expect? ▼
3 Mbps (30min) up to 20 Mbps (Monthly). Speeds shared across connected users.

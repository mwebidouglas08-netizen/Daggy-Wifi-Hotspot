❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How do I connect to WiFi? ▼
Select a package, enter your M-Pesa number, confirm the payment prompt on your phone, and you'll be automatically connected. It takes less than 30 seconds.
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
We accept M-Pesa mobile payments. The STK push is sent directly to your phone — just enter your M-Pesa PIN to complete payment.
What happens when my package expires? ▼
Your internet access will stop automatically. You'll be redirected here to purchase a new package. All your data and browsing history remains on your device.
Can I share my connection? ▼
Each package is tied to your phone number and device. Hotspot sharing may affect your speed. For multiple devices, consider the Weekly or Monthly package.
I was charged but no internet — what do I do? ▼
Check your M-Pesa messages for a confirmation SMS with a reference code. Go to the Reconnect tab, enter that code and click Verify & Connect. If the issue persists, contact support with your M-Pesa reference number.
What speeds can I expect? ▼
Speeds depend on your package and current network load. Our basic 1-hour plan offers up to 5 Mbps download, while the Monthly plan offers up to 20 Mbps. Speeds are shared across all connected users.
